Overview of the city


Aarhus is located in a natural bay along a small river and surrounded by forests. The young population and the fact that the city is the unofficial “capital of west Denmark”means that Aarhus has a large concentration of attractions, shopping and city life.

Although it is a big city it has a lot of beaches,forests and all types of outdoor activities. The city is really colorful . Make sure you check our social media accounts to keep track on the best places to visit right before you arrive:

The city inspires with its beauty and architecture as well as the impressive amount of green zones and parks.
